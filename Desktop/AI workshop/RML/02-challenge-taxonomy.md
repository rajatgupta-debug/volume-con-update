## Launch Set Selection Criteria

A challenge makes the Launch 100 if it meets **all five** criteria:

1. **High wow-factor.** The hook must generate a genuine "wait, what?" reaction. Test: would a 10-year-old voluntarily read the hook aloud to a parent?
2. **High shareability.** The reveal must be interesting enough that a kid would tell a friend, even without a share button. Test: is the completion card something a parent would post on social media?
3. **Real-world authenticity.** The connection to the real world must be genuine, not contrived. Test: would a working professional in that field recognize the math as accurate?
4. **Theme and level balance.** The Launch 100 must cover all 10 themes and all 7 levels, with the distribution roughly matching the matrix above.
5. **Production feasibility.** The challenge must be buildable with text, images, and standard interactive elements — no custom simulations or 3D rendering for launch.
