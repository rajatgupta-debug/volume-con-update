Volume Con · What You Found
Same price. Less product. Every time.

The Shrinkflation Scam
Radius is squared. A 10% radius cut removes 19% of volume — not 10%.
2
The Base Squeeze
Two cuts multiply. 8% off each side of the base removes 15% total.
3
The Hollow Illusion
A cone at 80% height holds only 51% of its volume. The bottom is almost empty.
The Pattern
Why
Volume formulas are non-linear. Small dimension cuts cause bigger volume losses than they appear.
How
Always check the fl oz or oz on the label — not the shape, the price, or how full it looks.
